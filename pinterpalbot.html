PinterPal bot Explained

Our product is an intelligent widget designed to help customers find the perfect product match through a personalized questionnaire. This widget, which can be integrated into e-commerce websites via API (offered as a monthly subscription), guides users step-by-step through a tailored set of questions about their specific needs and preferences. The questionnaire, typically between 5-10 questions, adapts dynamically based on each user’s responses, leading them to the product(s) that best meets their criteria.

For instance, if a customer is searching for a vacuum cleaner, the widget will ask targeted questions—whether they prefer a cordless model, quiet operation, bagless design, affordability, etc. By understanding these preferences, the widget narrows down the options to present the best-suited products.

Our AI-driven system generates these questions using data from the webshop itself, including product descriptions, specifications, and other relevant details thst can be found on the website. This makes the experience highly customized, with each survey tailored to the unique offerings and inventory of the business. Whether for electronics, home goods, vacations, or other categories, the widget provides a seamless, guided shopping experience that benefits both the customer and the retailer.
